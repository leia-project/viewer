Projects Tool Help

Welcome to the Projects Tool help section. This guide will walk you through the essential functionalities to get you started with managing your projects effectively.

Here's how to use it:

  1. Creating a project: To begin, locate and click the "New Project" button. A new project entry will instantly appear, ready for you to input its details and tasks.
  2. Adding tasks: Once you have a project, you can add individual tasks to it. Simply enter the task description into the designated field and press Enter.
  3. Saving: Your progress is automatically saved as you add or modify tasks, ensuring that you never lose any of your valuable work.
  4. Editing a task: To make changes to an existing task, double-click on the task item. An editable input field will appear, allowing you to update the task description.
  5. Deleting a task: To remove a task, double-click on the task to activate its edit mode, and then click the "Delete" button. Confirm your action if prompted.

For further assistance, detailed documentation, or to report an issue, please do not hesitate to contact our support team at support@example.com.
